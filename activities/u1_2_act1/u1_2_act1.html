Unit 1.2 · Activity 1 · Rally CoachSolution 11

Solution 11

Compare strategies
Two students evaluate $3(8+4)-5$. One distributes first and one simplifies inside the parentheses first. Show both methods and explain why they agree.
Solution: Inside first: $3(8+4)-5=3(12)-5=36-5=31$. Distribute first: $3(8)+3(4)-5=24+12-5=31$. They agree because distribution rewrites an equivalent expression.

Solution 16

Justify linear pattern
The outputs are $2, 6, 10, 14$ for inputs $0,1,2,3$. Explain how you know the pattern has constant change and write a rule.
Solution: The output increases by $4$ each time the input increases by $1$, so the change is constant. Since the output at $x=0$ is $2$, a rule is $y=4x+2$.
