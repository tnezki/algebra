Unit 1.4 · Activity 2Exit Ticket Answer Key

Exit Ticket Answer Key

Expressions
1. Find and fix the error: $15x+10=5(15x+10)$.
Expressions
2. Find and fix the error: $4(x+3)=4x+3$.
Equations
3. Find and fix the error: $3x+5=20$ → $3x=25$ → $x=\dfrac{25}{3}$.
Equations
4. Find and fix the error: $4x+7=x+22$ → $4x=15$ → $x=\dfrac{15}{4}$.
Linear rules
5. Find and fix the error: starting value $8$, rate $-3$ gives $y=8x-3$.
Linear rules
6. Find and fix the error for $y=2x+1$: outputs for $x=0,1,2$ are $2,3,4$.
Teacher key:
  1. $15x+10=5(3x+2)$; the student forgot to divide each term inside by the GCF.
  2. $4(x+3)=4x+12$; the $4$ must multiply both terms inside the parentheses.
  3. $3x+5=20$ → $3x=15$ → $x=5$; subtract $5$, do not add $5$.
  4. $4x+7=x+22$ → $3x+7=22$ → $3x=15$ → $x=5$; subtract $x$ from both sides first.
  5. $y=-3x+8$; the rate is the coefficient of $x$ and the starting value is the constant.
  6. The outputs are $1,3,5$; substitute $0,1,2$ into $y=2x+1$.
