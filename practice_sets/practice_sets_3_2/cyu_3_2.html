Check Your Understanding 3.2 — Solving Systems by Substitution
1.Unit 3.2 | Formative | Substitute correctly

For the system $y=2x-1$ and $x+y=14$, write the equation that results after substituting for $y$.

2.Unit 3.2 | Formative | Solve system

$$y=2x-1$$

$$x+y=14$$

3.Unit 3.2 | Formative | Substitute for x

Solve using substitution.

$$x=y+5$$

$$2x+y=19$$

5.Unit 3.2 | Formative | Graph connection
