Unit 4.1 — Recognizing Function Families
1.Unit 4.1 | DoK 1 | Graph recognition

Use Sort Set B. Which graphs show a V-shape, a U-shape, a curve with an endpoint, and two separated branches?

2.Unit 4.1 | DoK 2 | Equation to family

Classify each equation and state one feature you expect in its graph.

  1. $y=\sqrt{x}+1$
  2. $y=\frac{4}{x}$
  3. $y=|x-3|+2$
3.Unit 4.1 | DoK 3 | Table critique

4.Unit 4.1 | DoK 4 | Create a sort

Unit 3.5 Spiral — Systems of Linear Inequalities

6.Unit 3.5 | DoK 1 | Constraint count

7.Unit 3.5 | DoK 1 | Nonnegative constraints

9.Unit 3.5 | DoK 2 | Test point

$$x\ge 0$$

$$y\ge 0$$

$$2x+3y\le 18$$

11.Unit 3.5 | DoK 3 | Error analysis

12.Unit 3.5 | DoK 4 | Decision
